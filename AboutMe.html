About Me

In my early years I loved playing video games much like most everyone else and was facinated on how such things like video games and computer software was created. starting in early middle school, I would begin to explore different avenues of how I could make games and difeerent softwares. I would spend most of my free time learning and prototyping different projects, granted none of them were really any good but it is what started my passion for design and programming.

I graduated Highschool from Des Moines Highschool back in 2014 and soon after enrolled in the Game Design degree program at Full Sail University to expand on my knowledge of design and programming. I would graduate from Full Sail University in September of 2020 with a BA in Game Design. While attending Full Sail University I would Hone my abilityies not only in Game and Level design, but also UI design, UX Design, and Programming becoming Proficeint in C# and C++ visual coding.

Throughout and after college I have aquired experience working on project ranging from website creation and utilitys, to video games. I am proficient in languages such as HTML5, Javascript, SQL, and C#, as well as visual design in CSS. I have also had experience in game engines Unity and Unreal Engine where I would build my game projects.
